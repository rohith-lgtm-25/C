Important Concept: DDA Algorithm

This function uses the Digital Differential Analyzer (DDA) algorithm.

Step 1
dx = abs(x2 - x1);
dy = abs(y2 - y1);

Example:

(2,3) -> (8,7)

then

dx = 6
dy = 4
Step 2
steps = (dx > dy) ? dx : dy;

Choose the larger distance.

steps = 6
Step 3
xin = (x2 - x1)/steps;
yin = (y2 - y1)/steps;

Increment amount.

xin = 1
yin = 0.66
Step 4

Move point by point.

x += xin;
y += yin;

and mark

pic[y][x]='*';

These are the same operations used in databases and web applications.

6. Re-rendering Concept
render_all();

Instead of changing pixels directly, the program redraws the whole scene from stored data.

This is exactly how many GUI frameworks, games, and even React work conceptually:

Data Changes
      ↓
Re-render
      ↓
Updated Screen
